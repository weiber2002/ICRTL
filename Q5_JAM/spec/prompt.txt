
**TASK:** Implement a synthesizable Verilog module **`JAM`** that finds the **minimum total assignment cost** and the **number of assignments** achieving it for **8 workers √ó 8 jobs** by **exhaustively enumerating all permutations** and reading costs from a synchronous 8√ó8 ROM via `(W,J)`. **Do not change any port names or widths.**

### I/O (fixed; do not change)

* `input  CLK` ‚Äî posedge synchronous
* `input  RST` ‚Äî asynchronous active-high; clears state; **testbench holds RST High for \~2 cycles before deasserting**; no `Valid` while High
* `output reg [2:0] W` ‚Äî worker index 0..7
* `output reg [2:0] J` ‚Äî job index 0..7
* `input  [6:0] Cost` ‚Äî unsigned cost 0..100 from ROM
* `output reg [3:0] MatchCount` ‚Äî number of min-cost assignments (0..15)
* `output reg [9:0] MinCost` ‚Äî unsigned min total cost (‚â§1024)
* `output reg Valid` ‚Äî **1-cycle pulse** when `MinCost/MatchCount` are final

### Cost ROM protocol

* Apply `(W,J)` during cycle **t**; sample the corresponding `Cost` at the **next rising edge (t+1)** (synchronous read).
* **Keep `W` and `J` stable throughout the cycle they are driven to meet setup/hold.**
* Reads are **side-effect free**; the same entry may be read any number of times.

### Required behavior

1. Generate **all 8! permutations** of `[0..7]`. Treat a permutation **P** as the mapping `W=k ‚Üí J=P[k]`.
2. For each permutation, compute
   `TotalCost = Œ£_{k=0..7} Cost(W=k, J=P[k])` using the ROM.
3. Track `MinCost` (minimum of all totals) and `MatchCount` (how many totals equal that minimum).
4. When all permutations are processed, drive `MinCost` and `MatchCount`, and assert `Valid=1` for **exactly one** clock. The testbench terminates on the **next** cycle.

### Next-permutation (reference method; any correct exhaustive method is OK)

* **Pivot:** find rightmost `i` with `a[i] < a[i+1]`.
* **Successor:** in the suffix, pick the smallest `a[j] > a[i]`.
* **Swap + reverse:** swap `a[i], a[j]`, then reverse `a[i+1..end]`.
* Start from `[0,1,2,3,4,5,6,7]` and iterate until all permutations are visited.

### Ranges & types

* `Cost`: 7-bit unsigned (0..100).
* `MinCost`: 10-bit unsigned (‚â§1024).
* `MatchCount`: 4-bit unsigned.
* `W`, `J`: 3-bit unsigned (0..7).

### Constraints

* No pattern-specific shortcuts or precomputed answers; **must** truly enumerate and compute.
* **Do not modify** port names or widths; keep timing and `Valid` protocol exactly as specified.
* While `RST=1`, clear all state; begin operation only after deassertion.
